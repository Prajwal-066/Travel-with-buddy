About Travel with Buddy

Our mission is to revolutionize the way people travel by providing a safe, reliable, and convenient ride-sharing platform that connects passengers with trusted buddies who are also travelling to the same destination.

Founded in 2025, Travel with Buddy has a vision to become one of the most trusted ride-sharing platforms in India, serving thousands of customers daily across major cities.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us to create the perfect ride-sharing experience

Safety First

We prioritize the safety of our passengers and drivers with thorough background checks, real-time tracking, and 24/7 support.

Eco-Friendly

By promoting ride-sharing, we're committed to reducing traffic congestion and carbon emissions for a cleaner environment.

Community Focus

We build communities by connecting people and creating economic opportunities for drivers while making transportation accessible to all.
